## Colors
**Blue** is our primary color, while **yellow** and **red** should be used as accent colors with care.
### Blue [#002868]
* HEX: #002868
* RGB: (0,40,104)
* CMYK: (100%, 92%, 30%, 23%)
* Pantone: 280 C
### Yellow [#FFCA00]
* HEX: #FFCA00
* RGB: (255,202,0)
* CMYK: (0%, 20%, 100%, 0%)
* Pantone: 116 C
### Red [#BF0A30]
* HEX: #BF0A30
* RGB: (191,10,48)
* CMYK: (17%, 100%, 86%, 7%)
* Pantone: 200 C
